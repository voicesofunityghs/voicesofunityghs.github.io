Club Information

What We Do

  • Learn new content about languages from all across the globe.
  • Have in-depth discussions about dialects and cultures.
  • Watch movies and documentaries relating to language.
  • Occasionally have snacks!

Additional Topics

  • Discuss the contributions of languages in history (ex: Navajo Code Talkers)
  • Explore linguistics (ex: phonetics)
  • Understand issues with language death and its impact on humanity.

Get Involved

Whether you're fluent in multiple languages or just curious about learning something new, there's a place for you in the Voices of Unity Club. Everyone is welcome to explore, share, and celebrate culture together.
